Our Lawn Care Services

We provide lawn cutting and basic outdoor maintenance services. Services are available as one-time bookings or scheduled maintenance plans.

Service List

Standard Lawn Mowing

Grass cutting for residential and small commercial lawns.

Edge Trimming

Trimming around fences, driveways, garden borders, and corners.

Weed Control (Basic)

Removal of weeds using trimming or manual cleanup methods.

Green Waste Removal

Collection and removal of grass clippings and garden waste.

Seasonal Cleanup

Leaf clearing, garden tidying, and general outdoor cleanup.

Scheduled Maintenance

Weekly, fortnightly, or monthly lawn cutting plans.

Pricing Notes

Pricing depends on lawn size, grass height, accessibility, and required cleanup time. Quotes can be provided based on photos or on-site inspection.

Booking Requirements

  • Clear access to the lawn area
  • Remove large objects (toys, branches, stones)
  • Pets should be secured during service
